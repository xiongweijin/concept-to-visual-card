# Style Presets

Use these as starting points. Adjust to match the concept, audience, and platform.

## Clean Editorial Card

Keywords: clean editorial design, refined grid, soft off-white background, restrained color accents, clear hierarchy, elegant diagram elements.

Best for: 朋友圈, 公众号, general knowledge sharing.

## Warm Sketch Explainer

Keywords: hand-drawn pencil sketch, warm paper texture, light crosshatching, human touch, thoughtful and accessible.

Best for: philosophy, learning notes, reflective concepts.

## Flat Knowledge Graphic

Keywords: modern flat illustration, geometric shapes, limited color palette, crisp icons, friendly educational design.

Best for: tech, business, product concepts.

## Retro Comic Card

Keywords: retro comic style, bold outlines, halftone dots, expressive panels, energetic composition.

Best for: psychology, social concepts, humorous reversals.

## Ink Wash Minimal

Keywords: Chinese ink wash, rice paper texture, minimalist brush strokes, negative space, quiet composition.

Best for: Eastern philosophy, literature, cultural ideas.

## Tech Diagram

Keywords: precise diagram, subtle grid, clean arrows, modular nodes, contemporary interface aesthetics.

Best for: AI, software, systems, mechanisms.

## Text Legibility Rules

- Use large, clear Chinese text.
- Keep each text block short.
- Avoid more than 5 explanation blocks in one image.
- Do not place text on visually noisy areas.
- Prefer labels and callouts over paragraphs.
